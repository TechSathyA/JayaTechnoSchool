Admission process for 2026-27

We aim to make the admission journey easy and informative for parents.

  1. Submit an enquiry form or call the admissions office.
  2. Attend a school interaction or campus visit.
  3. Understand curriculum, facilities, and class availability.
  4. Complete the application with required documents.
  5. Receive admission confirmation and onboarding guidance.
Seats are limited in selected grades. Early enquiries are recommended.
